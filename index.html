Privacy Policy (English)

1. Controller

The controller for the processing described in this app is Pedro Peraza Gonzalez, Eichendorffstraße 23, 57482 Wenden, Germany, email: peraza.pedr@gmail.com.

2. Contact

If you have questions about data protection, you can contact peraza.pedr@gmail.com.

3. General information about the app and scope

SolarScope is a mobile app used to display solar, geomagnetic and radio-related measurement or forecast data and to provide local converter and location-based features. These notices apply to the app itself. External websites and third-party providers are subject to their own privacy terms.

4. What data may be processed when using the app

Depending on the feature used, the app may process technical connection data for network requests, optional location data, search terms and coordinates for place search or reverse geocoding, user-entered content such as a callsign, locator or place name, and locally stored settings, cache and history data.

Based on the current state of the app, there are no user accounts, no login function, no user profiles, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs and no separate crash-reporting SDK in use.

5. App permissions

According to the current app setup, the app uses Internet access and may request location access when you actively use location-based features. Location access is feature-specific and is not required for general use of the app.

6. Local storage on the device

The app stores several items locally on the device, including an optional callsign, UI and convenience settings, local caches for solar and ionosonde data, local trend history, geocoding caches and Local Radio Assessment settings including a manual locator and the last locally saved current position.

According to the current app behavior, these local data are not automatically uploaded to the operator. Where no fixed retention period exists in the app logic, the data remain stored until overwritten, deleted in the app, or removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

7. Use of external data sources and third parties

The app directly requests content from external data sources. Depending on the feature used, these may include HAMQSL, Juliusruh / IAP / GIRO DIDBase, NOAA SWPC, Open-Meteo and Nominatim / OpenStreetMap. When such direct requests are made, the relevant providers may process technically required connection data in order to answer the request.

8. Location data / altitude feature / current position

Location data are processed only if you actively use location-based features and grant the relevant permission. General use of the app is possible without location access. According to the current app behavior, location is not tracked in the background.

The altitude feature is not purely local. GPS or system values may be processed locally, but the app may also transmit current coordinates to Open-Meteo for online terrain elevation fallback or cross-checking. The app operator does not use this feature to create movement profiles.

9. Geocoding / place search / nearest place

For place search and reverse geocoding, the app may use Nominatim at nominatim.openstreetmap.org. Depending on the feature, place names, search terms, coordinates, language settings and technical request metadata may be transmitted. Results may be cached locally on the device.

10. Juliusruh / ionosonde / HAMQSL / other radio-related data sources

SolarScope uses external specialist data sources exclusively to display scientific, technical and radio-related measurement or forecast data in the app.

Important: The app operator does not sell personal data. The app does not engage in data trading. Juliusruh, GIRO, HAMQSL, NOAA and comparable sources are used as specialist or measurement data sources for display, not as purchased personal user data. The app operator does not instruct these sources to market users' personal data for advertising or sale.

The website prop.kc2g.com/stations/ linked in the app is only an external link and not a data source actively fetched by the app.

11. External links inside the app

The app contains external links, for example to HAMQSL, prop.kc2g.com, Juliusruh / IAP, OpenStreetMap attribution pages and the browser version of this privacy policy. If you open such a link, a direct connection will be established between your device and the relevant third-party provider.

12. Legal bases for processing

Where personal data or data linkable to a person are processed, the current assessment mainly relies on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR for app features actively requested by you and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR for technically necessary local storage, caching, UI persistence and reliable app operation. Email enquiries may rely on Art. 6(1)(b) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR depending on the content of the enquiry.

13. Recipients / categories of recipients

Depending on the feature used, data may be transmitted to external data sources and API providers, to providers of external websites, or to involved email providers. According to the current app structure, there is no own user database and no dedicated backend service for these processing activities.

14. International transfers

For third-party providers accessed directly by the app, it cannot be ruled out that data may also be processed outside the EU / EEA. This may in particular apply to providers located in the United States. Direct external links and direct external requests remain subject to the privacy practices of the respective provider.

15. Storage period / deletion

According to the current app logic, locally stored settings, caches, history values, callsign data, a stored manual locator and a locally stored last current position remain on the device until they are overwritten, deleted in the app, removed by clearing app data, or removed by uninstalling the app. Runtime-only values are held only for the duration of active use.

16. Security

Based on the current implementation, the app attempts to use HTTPS for external requests wherever supported by the relevant provider. However, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

17. Rights of data subjects

Where the GDPR applies, you may in particular have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection. For direct third-party requests, the relevant third-party provider may also need to be contacted.

18. Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of your habitual residence, place of work or the place of the alleged infringement.

19. Requirement to provide data

There is no legal or contractual obligation to provide personal data for general use of the app. However, some data may be technically required if you want to use specific features, for example location data for location-based functions, search terms for place search, coordinates for reverse geocoding or a callsign for optional local display in the app.

20. Children and young people

The app is not specifically directed at children. The current app design does not indicate any deliberate collection of children's personal data.

3. Allgemeines zur App und zum Geltungsbereich

Reverse-Geocoding, vom Nutzer eingegebene Inhalte wie Callsign, Locator oder Ortsbezeichnungen sowie lokal gespeicherte Einstellungs-, Cache- und Verlaufsdaten.

Nach aktuellem App-Stand werden keine Nutzerkonten, keine Login-Funktion, keine Nutzerprofile, keine Werbe-SDKs, keine Analytics-SDKs und kein gesondertes Crash-Reporting-SDK eingesetzt.

6. Lokale Speicherung auf dem Endgerät

Die App speichert lokal auf dem Endgerät insbesondere ein optionales Callsign, UI- und Komforteinstellungen, lokale Caches für Solar- und Ionosonde-Daten, lokale Verlaufsdaten, Geocoding-Caches sowie Einstellungen der Local-Radio-Assessment-Funktion einschließlich eines manuellen Locators und der zuletzt lokal gespeicherten aktuellen Position.

Diese Daten werden nach aktuellem App-Verhalten nicht automatisch an den Betreiber hochgeladen. Soweit keine feste Frist vorgesehen ist, bleiben sie gespeichert, bis sie überschrieben, in der App gelöscht, durch Löschen der App-Daten entfernt oder durch Deinstallation beseitigt werden.

Die App ruft Inhalte direkt von externen Datenquellen ab. Je nach genutzter Funktion können dazu insbesondere HAMQSL, Juliusruh / IAP / GIRO DIDBase, NOAA SWPC, Open-Meteo sowie Nominatim / OpenStreetMap gehören. Bei solchen direkten Abrufen können die jeweiligen Anbieter technisch erforderliche Verbindungsdaten verarbeiten, um die Anfrage zu beantworten.

8. Standortdaten / Höhenfunktion / aktuelle Position

Standortdaten werden nur verarbeitet, wenn Sie standortbezogene Funktionen aktiv nutzen und die entsprechende Freigabe erteilen. Die allgemeine Nutzung der App ist auch ohne Standortzugriff möglich. Nach aktuellem App-Verhalten erfolgt keine Standortverfolgung im Hintergrund.

Die Höhenfunktion ist nicht ausschließlich lokal. GPS- oder Systemwerte können lokal verarbeitet werden; zusätzlich kann die App aktuelle Koordinaten an Open-Meteo übermitteln, um eine Online-Geländehöhe zu bestimmen oder Werte abzugleichen. Die Funktion dient nicht dazu, Bewegungsprofile zu erstellen.
