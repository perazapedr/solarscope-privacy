1. What this policy covers

This policy explains how SolarScope handles information when you use the app to view solar/space-weather and HF propagation data.

2. Summary

  • We do not collect, sell, or share personal data from users.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • The app makes network requests to third-party data sources to display content; those providers may receive your device’s IP address and standard request metadata to deliver the data.

3. Data we process

No personal data collected by us. SolarScope has no accounts, no login, no user profiles, no cookies, and no device-identifier tracking.

Connection data (via third parties). When the app fetches content from external sources (e.g., hamqsl.com, prop.kc2g.com), those providers inevitably receive technical connection data (IP address, user agent). We do not control their logs or policies.

When you contact us. If you email us, we will process your message solely to respond and delete it when no longer needed.

4. Permissions

SolarScope requires only Internet access. No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or background activity permissions are requested.

5. Data storage & retention

No personal data is stored by the app or us. Any cached data is non-personal and short-lived.

6. Children’s privacy

Not directed to children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect children’s data.

7. International transfers

External providers may operate servers outside your country. We do not control those infrastructures.

8. Security

All app requests use HTTPS where supported. We do not maintain user databases or profiles.

9. Your rights

Because we do not collect user data, typical rights (access, deletion, portability) generally do not apply. If you contact us and provide data in that context, you can request deletion via the contact email.

10. Third-party links

We are not responsible for content or privacy practices of third-party sites.
